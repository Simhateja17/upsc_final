<objective>
Connect content and planning screens (Videos, Library, Study Planner, Syllabus Tracker) to backend APIs, enabling real content browsing and study planning.
</objective>

<execution_context>
Depends on Plan 01 (ApiClient, ServiceLocator) and Plan 02 (patterns). Maps to backend `/videos/*`, `/library/*`, `/study-plan/*`, `/syllabus` routes.
</execution_context>

<context>
Backend routes:
- `/videos` → list video lectures
- `/videos/:id` → video details
- `/library` → list library items (PDFs, notes)
- `/library/:id` → item details / download
- `/study-plan` → get user's study plan
- `/study-plan/tasks` → get tasks
- `/study-plan/tasks/:id/complete` → mark task complete
- `/syllabus` → get syllabus structure
- `/syllabus/:subject` → get subject topics
</context>
